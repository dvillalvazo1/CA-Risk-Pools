# California-Incorporated-Cities-Towns
Raw files, scripts, and cleaned data for Debora Villalvazo's research on public risk pools and private insurers for California cities and towns. 

**California Joint Powers Insurance Authority Data (CJPIA)**: Raw membership data from the Way Back Machine. Will help detect which cities belong to the public risk pool (California Joint Powers Insurance Authority) at different points in time. 
  Raw file: "risk_pool.txt" 
  .Rmd: "CJPIA.Rmd" 
  Output file: "CJPIA.csv" 

**Public Entity Risk Management Authority (PERMA)**: Raw membership data from their website: Will help detect which cities belong to the public risk pool at different points in time. 
  Raw file: "perma.csv" 
  .Rmd: "perma.Rmd"
  Output file: "permadata.csv"

**Public Risk Innovation, Solutions, and Management (PRISM)** Note: This risk pool provides two distinct general liability coverage plans: General Liability 1:
“The General Liability 1 (GL1) Program utilizes a combination of pooling and purchasing of excess coverage which gives you the ability to respond to changing market conditions, and has made it one of the largest and most successful pooling programs in the United States.” AND general liability 2: “The General Liability 2 (GL2) Program was specifically designed to offer larger members the advantages of the joint purchase of excess coverage while maintaining your ability to handle and fund primary losses.”
